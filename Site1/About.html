About Me

My name is Praveen Chakkadi, and I'm a Cloud Consultant, Author, and Thinker. I love building and creating innovative Cloud virtualized architecture projects and designing characters, infusing their lives with my words, enhancing their inherent strength and power by making them believe in the characters that lived before. That's what makes me an Author. When I was younger, I never imagined that I would write something promising to believe in. I simply thought I would create art for the sake of my free time. Now, as a Cloud consultant and Author, I realize the power of being an IT employee and Author. Life needs experiences, not just a chance to live.


-Praveen Chakkadi
